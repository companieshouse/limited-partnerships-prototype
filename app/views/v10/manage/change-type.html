Apply for designation as a private fund limited partnership (PFLP)

A limited partnership can be designated as a PFLP if it is constituted by an agreement in writing and is a collective investment scheme.

Becoming a PFLP means that:

  • Limited partners will no longer be required to contribute capital
  • Limited partners can appoint a person to wind up the partnership if there is no general partner available to do so
  • Partners can make an agreement about winding up the partnership
  • Limited partners may take more actions without risking losing their limited liability status
  • Some administrative requirements will be removed
  • The partnership will no longer qualify as a ‘partnership scheme’, and therefore cannot be an authorised contractual scheme under section 235A(6)(aa) of the Financial Services and Markets Act 2000

Find more information in the Legislative Reform (Private Fund Limited Partnerships) Order 2017.

It costs £30 to apply.
