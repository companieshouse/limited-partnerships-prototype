You must be a Companies House authorised agent to use this service

You cannot continue with this service, because the email you have signed in with is not linked to an Authorised Corporate Service Provider (ACSP) account.

Only ACSPs, also known as Companies House authorised agents, can file for limited partnerships.

Authorised agents are businesses that are registered with a UK Anti-Money Laundering (AML) supervisory body. For example, accountants, solicitors or company formation agents. They have registered with Companies House to be able to file on behalf of clients.

If you work for a business that's already registered as an authorised agent

You'll need to sign in with an email address that's already linked to the account, or ask a colleague whose email address is linked to add you as a user.

If you are an agent, but have not yet registered as a Companies House authorised agent

You'll need to apply to register as an authorised agent.

If you are someone else involved with the limited partnership

You'll need to find a Companies House authorised agent to file on your behalf.
