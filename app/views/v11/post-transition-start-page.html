File for a limited partnership

Use this service to view or update information about a limited partnership, or to file a confirmation statement for a limited partnership.

Who can use this service

To file for a limited partnership, you must be an Authorised Corporate Service Provider (ACSP). ACSPs are also known as Companies House authorised agents.

Authorised agents are businesses like accountants, solicitors or company formation agents, that have registered with Companies House to be able to file on behalf of clients. Find out more about registering as an authorised agent.

If you are someone else involved with the limited partnership, you'll need to find a Companies House authorised agent to file on your behalf.

Anyone can use this service to view information about limited partnerships on the public register.

Before you file

If you're filing for a limited partnership that was registered before XX DATE, you must provide new required information first.

Types of filings you can make

This service has replaced paper forms LP6, LP8 and SLP CS01.

You can make the following filings for a limited partnership within this service:

  • update the partnership’s name
  • update the registered office address or principal place of business
  • update the registered email address
  • designate a limited partnership as a private fund limited partnership (PFLP)
  • add or remove partners
  • update information about partners
  • file a confirmation statement
  • update SIC codes (this must be done within the confirmation statement)
  • close the partnership

If you need to update information about people with significant control (PSCs), you’ll need to find and complete the relevant form. File it online using our Upload a document to Companies House service.

What you'll need

You’ll need:

  • to sign in to your Companies House account
  • the limited partnership number

We charge a fee for:

  • updating the partnership’s name
  • designation as a private fund limited partnership (PFLP)
  • filing a confirmation statement (unless you've already paid a confirmation statement fee during this payment period)

If there is a fee, you'll need a credit or debit card.
