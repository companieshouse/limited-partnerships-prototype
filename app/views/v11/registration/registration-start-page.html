Register a limited partnership

Use this service to register a limited partnership, or a private fund limited partnership.

  • From: Companies House

Heading 1

You can register a partnership in Scotland, or in England, Wales or Northern Ireland.

This service has replaced the forms to register a:

  • limited partnership (LP5)
  • private fund limited partnership (LP7)
  • limited partnership in Scotland (LP5(s))
  • private fund limited partnership in Scotland (LP7(s))

Heading 2

You must be a Companies House authorised agent to use this service.

Only Authorised Corporate Service Providers (ACSPs) can register limited partnerships. ACSPs are also known as Companies House authorised agents.

Authorised agents are businesses like accountants, solicitors or company formation agents, that have registered with Companies House to be able to file on behalf of clients. Find out more about registering as an authorised agent.

If you are someone else involved with the limited partnership, you'll need to find a Companies House authorised agent to file on your behalf.

Heading 3

You must check the limited partnership’s name meets certain rules. We may reject your application if, for example, the name is the same as another registered partnership or company.

Read the guidance on choosing a company name to find out what rules the name must meet.

What you'll need

You’ll need to give details about the limited partnership, including:

  • the registered office address
  • the address of the principal place of business
  • the registered email address
  • the term of the partnership, if relevant
  • a Standard Industrial Classification (SIC) code to describe the nature of business, if relevant
  • information about all general and limited partners
Read the guidance on registering a limited partnership to check you have all the information you'll need.

You'll need a credit or debit card to pay the £XX registration fee.

You'll need to sign in to use this service. You can save your answers and resume your application later if you need to.
