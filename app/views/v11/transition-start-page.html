Provide new required information about a limited partnership

Use this service to provide more information about a limited partnership to confirm it is still active, and to meet new requirements.

Heading 1

All limited partnerships that were registered between XX DATE and XX DATE must do this. This is due to measures introduced by the Economic Crime and Corporate Transparency (ECCT) Act.

The transitional period to allow existing partnerships to meet the new requirements will end on XX DATE. If a limited partnership does not provide an update by this date, we will dissolve it and remove it from the register.

Heading 2

You must be a Companies House authorised agent to use this service

Only Authorised Corporate Service Providers (ACSPs) can provide this information about a limited partnership. ACSPs are also known as Companies House authorised agents.

Authorised agents are businesses like accountants, solicitors or company formation agents, that have registered with Companies House to be able to file on behalf of clients. Find out more about registering as an authorised agent.

If you are someone else involved with the limited partnership, you'll need to find a Companies House authorised agent to file on your behalf.

What you'll need

You'll need to provide:

  • the limited partnership number
  • a registered email address
  • a registered office address - this must be in the jurisdiction where the partnership was formed
  • information about all general and limited partners

You'll need to sign in to use this service.

You can save your answers and resume your application later if you need to.
