You must be an Authorised Corporate Service Provider (ACSP) to use this service

You cannot use this service, because the email you have signed in with is not linked to an ACSP account.

Only ACSPs can file for limited partnerships.

ACSPs are individuals or businesses that are registered with a UK Anti-Money Laundering (AML) supervisory body. For example, accountants, solicitors or company formation agents. They have registered with Companies House to be able to file on behalf of clients.

If you work for a business that is registered as an ACSP

Sign in using an email address that is linked to your business's ACSP account. If you don't have one, you'll need to speak to someone in your organisation with 'administrator' or 'account owner' access to the ACSP account.

If you're an agent, but have not yet registered as an ACSP

You or your business will need to register as an ACSP before you can file for a limited partnership.

If you're someone else involved with the limited partnership

You'll need to find an ACSP to file on your behalf.
