Updating limited partnership information

How to update information for a limited partnership.

You must tell Companies House about changes to your limited partnership.

If you're filing an update to a limited partnership that was registered before XX DATE, you must provide new required information first.

Who can update limited partnership information

You can only file for a limited partnership if you are an Authorised Corporate Service Provider (ACSP).

ACSPs are agents, such as accountants or solicitors, that have registered with Companies House to be able to file on behalf of clients. To register as an ACSP, they must be supervised for Anti-Money Laundering (AML) purposes. Find out more about registering as an ACSP.

If you are someone else involved with the limited partnership, you'll need to find an ACSP to file on your behalf.

What information you can update online

You can now file most updates online. This replaces our LP6 and LP8 paper forms.

You can use our online services to:

  • update a partnership’s name
  • update a registered office address or principal place of business
  • update a registered email address
  • designate a limited partnership as a private fund limited partnership (PFLP)
  • add or remove partners
  • update information about partners

To update the partnership’s nature of business, also known as its SIC codes, you'll need to file a limited partnership confirmation statement.

Updating information about people with significant control (PSCs)

To update information about PSCs, you need to find and complete the relevant form. You can file it online using our Upload a document to Companies House service.

How to file updates online

To file an update online:

  1. Search for the partnership on the Companies House register
  2. Select ‘File for this company’ to sign in to your Companies House account
  3. Select the ‘Update’ link next to the information you need to update

You need to sign in using an email address that is linked to an ASCP account.

Fees

We charge a fee for some filings:

  • updating the partnership’s name – £X
  • designation as a private fund limited partnership (PFLP) – £X

If there is a fee, you'll need a credit or debit card.
