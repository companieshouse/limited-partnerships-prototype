You need to register as a Companies House authorised agent

To file an update for a limited partnership, your business must be registered as a Companies House authorised agent. This is also known as an authorised corporate service provider (ACSP).

If you work for a business that's already registered as an authorised agent, you'll need to ask another user to add you to the account.

Check the public list of Companies House authorised agents to find out if your business is already registered.

Otherwise, you'll need to apply to register as an authorised agent.
