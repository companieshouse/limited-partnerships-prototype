Register a limited partnership

Use this service to register a limited partnership in England, Wales, Northern Ireland or Scotland.

You can apply to register a:

  • limited partnership
  • private fund limited partnership
  • Scottish qualifying partnership

Who can use this service

You, or the business you work for, must be registered as an authorised agent. Read the guidance on how to register as a Companies House authorised agent.

If you cannot register, you will need to find an authorised agent to file for you.

What you'll need

You’ll need to give details about the limited partnership, including:

  • a registered office address
  • a principal office address
  • a registered email address
  • the term of the partnership, if relevant
  • a Standard Industrial Classification (SIC) code to describe the nature of business
  • details about the general and limited partners
If a general partner is a person

They will need to verify their identity before you can register the limited partnership. You'll need to give their Companies House personal code as part of the registration.

You should read the guidance on registering a limited partnership to check you have all the information you'll need. This will vary depending on which type of partnership you need to register.
