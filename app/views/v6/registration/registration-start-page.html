Register a limited partnership

Use this service to apply to register a:

  • limited partnership in England, Wales or Northern Ireland
  • private fund limited partnership in England, Wales or Northern Ireland
  • Scottish limited partnership
  • private fund limited partnership in Scotland

Find out more about different types of limited partnership.

This service has replaced the paper forms LP5, LP5(s), LP7 and LP7(s).

Who can use this service

Only Companies House authorised agents (also known as Authorised Corporate Service Providers, or ACSPs) can register limited partnerships.

Authorised agents are businesses like accountants, solicitors or company formation agents, that have registered with Companies House to be able to file on behalf of clients.

Read the guidance to check if you can register as an authorised agent. If you cannot, you will need to find an authorised agent to file for you.

What you'll need

You’ll need to give details about the limited partnership, including:

  • a registered office address
  • the principal place of business
  • a registered email address
  • the term of the partnership, if relevant
  • a Standard Industrial Classification (SIC) code to describe the nature of business, if relevant
  • details about the general and limited partners
New requirements mean you now need to provide more information when you register a limited partnership. Before you start, you should read the guidance on registering a limited partnership to check you have all the information you'll need.
