You must be a Companies House authorised agent to upload these forms

Only Authorised Corporate Service Providers (ACSPs) can upload forms for Scottish limited partnerships. ACSPs are also known as Companies House authorised agents.

What is a Companies House authorised agent?

Authorised agents are businesses like accountants, solicitors or company formation agents, that have registered with Companies House to be able to file on behalf of clients.

Read the guidance to check if you can register as an authorised agent. If you cannot, you will need to find an authorised agent to file for you.
