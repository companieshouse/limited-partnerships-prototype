Upload a document to Companies House

Use this service to file certain documents online that would usually be sent to us in a paper format.

You can use this service to upload certain documents for:

  • share capital
  • registrar's powers
  • change of constitution
  • Scottish qualifying partnerships
If you need to upload documents for a Scottish limited partnership, or insolvency documents

Insolvency documents

You must be a registered insolvency practitioner to upload insolvency documents.

Read the guidance for uploading insolvency documents.

Documents for Scottish limited partnerships

Only Companies House authorised agents (also known as Authorised Corporate Service Provider, ACSPs) can upload documents for Scottish limited partnerships.

Read the guidance to check if you can register as an authorised agent.

What you'll need

You'll need:

  • to sign in to or create a Companies House account
  • the completed document or Companies House form
  • to save the completed document to the device you are using

For some document types, you'll need:

  • the company authentication code
  • to pay for the document
  • to include supporting evidence
