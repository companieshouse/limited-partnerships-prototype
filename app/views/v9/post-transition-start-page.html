Manage a limited partnership

Use this service to view or update information about a limited partnership, or to file a confirmation statement for a limited partnership.

This service has replaced paper forms LP6, LP8 and SLP CS01.

Who can use this service

Only Authorised Corporate Service Providers (ACSPs) can use this service. ACSPs are also known as Companies House authorised agents.

Authorised agents are businesses that are registered with a UK Anti-Money Laundering (AML) supervisory body. For example, accountants, solicitors or company formation agents. They must register as an authorised agent with Companies House to be able to file on behalf of clients.

To find an authorised agent, you should contact agents to check:

  • if they are registered as an authorised agent with Companies House, also known as an Authorised Corporate Service Provider (ACSP)
  • if they can offer the services you need
  • how much they charge for their services

What you'll need

You’ll need:

  • to sign in to your Companies House account (it must be linked to an authorised agent account)
  • the limited partnership number

We charge a fee for:

  • changing the name of the limited partnership
  • filing a confirmation statement (unless you've already paid a fee during this payment period)

You'll need a credit or debit card to pay these fees.
